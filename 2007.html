2.007 Robotics Competition

2.007 is MIT's Design and Manufacturing I and culminates in a final competition project

In this image is the final robot I competed with. I originally designed for the robot to actuate one specific challenge on the game board, where it could score about ~200 points and make it onto day two of the competition, but seeing as how day two of the competition coincided with the 2S.007 competition, I decided to go for a different challenge instead, netting about 70 points but being the only person to attempt that challenge that day.

The competition itself can be found at the tail end of this video, at the 6:30 mark. After the competition was over, the instructors allowed us to keep our robots, and I decided to turn my robot into a high energy robot, which can be seen in this video.
