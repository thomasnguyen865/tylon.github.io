First Combat Robots

I had joined the MIT Combat Robotics Club during the spring of my sophomore year, and got to work designing and making 3 lb combat robots.

My first combat robot I began designing with another person in the club, it was a big wheeled vertical spinner. The robot never ended up competing, as my teammate ended up leaving the club, so I began to individually design my own combat robot.

My actual first combat robot that I designed and competed with was called Applesauce, since it was waterjet from parts of an Apple computer
